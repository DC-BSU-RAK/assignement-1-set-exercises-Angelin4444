'Twinkling Watermelon' is a heartwarming family Korean drama with a mix of romantic comedy that blends time travel , music, and healing together. The story is about a young boy who is a CODA (both parents and his brother can't hear). He has a great passion for music, but he is living a dual life as a model student by day and plays the guitar in a band at night. His parents don't know he is a guitarist. One day his dad finds out, and they both get into a fight because of that. That night he discovers a musical store, which teleports him to the '90s when his father was the same age as him. There he got to know his father hadn't always been deaf . He tried to set everything right and tried to find a way back to the present. While on this journey, he understood the hardships and problems his parents faced when he was young , where he tried to change many of his parents fates. and that way of him understanding his parents made him closer to them, and he finally found out a way back to the present . When he returned, he himself saw many of the fates of his parents were changed.
